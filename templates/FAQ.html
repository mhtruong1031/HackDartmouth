FAQ

Why make FormFix?

We were inspired by Tony Stark's Jarvis technology of interacting with software through waving around in the air. From there, we looked for a cool use of mediapipe and ended up with FormFix.

How do I use the live camera feature?

Go to the "Live Video" page and allow camera access when prompted. The app will display your live video feed and analyze your form in real time. You can start or stop the camera at any time using the provided button.
